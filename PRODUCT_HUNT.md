## Full Description (for the listing body, ~260–400 words)

Unstructured Alpha scores 28 independent macro and alternative data signals every day — credit spreads, energy inventories, Fed liquidity, FINRA short interest, SEC Form 4 insider transactions, institutional 13F positioning, options flow, and more — and tells you what they're saying about the market right now.

**What it actually does:**

- **Signal Dashboard** — all 28 signals, live scores, bull/bear/neutral status, and trend direction. No paywall for browsing.
- **Ticker Deep Dive** — type any stock ticker. You get a Confluence Score (0–100), which signals are driving it, an auto-generated macro explanation, insider cluster detection, a 5-axis radar chart, and a full PDF report.
- **Today's Brief** — a daily macro briefing that reads like a Seeking Alpha article but is generated from live signal data, not editorial opinion.
- **Short Squeeze Radar** — screens for tickers with high short interest + macro tailwinds + insider buying. Updated daily.
- **Model Validation** — every score on the platform comes with its full validation status: out-of-sample backtest results, Bonferroni-corrected significance, and an honest "this hasn't held up" label when it hasn't. Most platforms hide this. This one doesn't.
- **Watchlist + Alerts** — set score thresholds for your tickers. Hourly cron checks for crossings and emails you.
- **Morning Digest Email** — daily 7 AM ET briefing with signal flips, score movers, and a macro editorial.

**What makes it different:**

The differentiation isn't the signals — it's the transparency. TipRanks' Smart Score and Seeking Alpha's Quant Rating disclose nothing about their methodology or validation. Unstructured Alpha publishes its exact backtest methodology, shows you when signals don't hold out-of-sample, and lets you run your own validation in the UI.

Built by one person. All data from public sources (FRED, SEC EDGAR, FINRA, EIA, yfinance). No proprietary data feeds. No black box.

Free tier: Signal Dashboard, Market Overview, Ticker Deep Dive (limited), Stock Screener.
Pro ($12/mo): Full Ticker Deep Dive, PDF exports, Signal Backtester, Factor Exposure, morning digest email, watchlist alerts.

## First Comment (maker's comment — post this yourself right after launch)

Hey PH! 👋 I'm Bhaumik, the solo builder behind Unstructured Alpha.

Quick origin story: I kept running into the same wall — platforms like TipRanks, Seeking Alpha Quant, and Bloomberg give you composite scores but tell you nothing about how they're computed, what data they use, or how well they've actually worked historically. A score you can't interrogate isn't a signal, it's a vibes number.

So I built this. Every signal on the platform traces to a real, public data source (FRED, SEC EDGAR, FINRA, EIA). Every composite score has a published validation status — including the ones that haven't held up in backtesting. That's intentional. A platform that shows you both the validated and the not-validated signals is a different kind of tool than one that hides the failures.

The Ticker Deep Dive is probably the best place to start — type any ticker and you'll see exactly which of the 28 macro signals are correlated with it historically, a 0–100 Confluence Score, auto-generated explanation, and the full signal-by-signal breakdown.

Happy to answer any questions about methodology, the tech stack (Streamlit + PostgreSQL + Render), or the signal construction.
